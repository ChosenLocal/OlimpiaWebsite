# SEO / AEO / GEO Playbook

- **AEO**: Answer-first (40–60 words) at top of pages; PAA-style Q&A with `FAQPage`.
- **GEO**: NAP consistency; GBP optimization; service-area pages with unique utility.
- **Classic SEO**: titles ≤ 60 chars, meta ≤ 155 chars; descriptive alt text; internal linking.
- **Schema**: LocalBusiness + Service + FAQ + Breadcrumb + SiteNavigationElement + Speakable.
- **Hreflang**: pair `/` with `/es/` equivalents.
